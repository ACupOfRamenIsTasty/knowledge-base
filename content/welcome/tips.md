# Tips
Make the most of your time at [Organization].

## Clarify expectations
- Regularly check-in with supervisors
- Frequently share progress and ask for guidance
- Confirm priorities and deadlines of tasks

## Learn through mistakes
- Be transparent about your knowledge
- Be comfortable not knowing everything
- Ask questions during meetings

## Build connections
- Get familiar with your team
- Observe behaviours of others

## Take initiative
- [Find a problem](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1YKDH-7ricvj6hZXpt3_Feg5SQw-nTYXy)
- [Solve it](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1I0UCc9zJGJLjmuciDhcvYKkLLsIPzPt2vM_BEiIzsD8)
- Don't wait for instructions

## See the bigger picture
- Understand how your work benefits [Organization]
- Know what problem you are solving
- Learn the start-to-finish development for your product
